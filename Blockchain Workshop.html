Blockchain Workshop

A basic to intermediate level introduction to Blockchain. The course will start with a very on introduction of blockchain and will progress through Ethereum, Introduction to solidity,Go Ethereum,oversight into hyperledger umberla projects and will endeavour into dapps development or Idea pitching workshop.
Target Topics :
  • Basics of Blockchain - History, Features, Consensus Protocols, Blockchain Versions
  • Technology Exposure - Basic Cryptographic Concepts, Ethereum World Computer
  • Overview of Blockchain - Various Blockchain networks
  • Intro Hyperledger Umbrella Projects - Fabric, Sawtooth
  • Advanced Blockchain - Dapps development or Idea pitching
Expected outcome of the Workshop:
An Intro to the vast world of blockchain, once the candidate goes through this goes he or she should be able to identify the potential use cases of blockchain and should be able to realise the point that “Blockchain isn’t a solution for the problems that doesn't exist, but a tool to overcome the challenges where it is ineluctable.”
Requirements for the Workshop :
  • Telegram Account
  • At Least 1 PC per 4 Students, OS: Windows 8.1 or Later
